Contributors are arranged into four different categories:

Chairperson
The chairperson is the individual responsible for oversight of all cookbook order functions. The chairperson is solely responsible for the submission of orders for production. By default, the person who signs up for an account is the chairperson.

Cochariperson
The cochairperson is the individual on record as the assistant to the chairperson. The cochairperson is only superseded by the chairperson.

Contributor
Contributors can add recipes and edit their own recipes.
