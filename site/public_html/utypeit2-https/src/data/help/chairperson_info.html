The Chairperson is the single person responsible for this cookbook project. The Chairperson holds the highest level of access to all parts of the account. There should only be one chairperson at a time

If you want to change Chairpersons for this project, first the Chairperson must reset their level by going to "Edit My Info" and selecting a different level of participation using the "Level" select menu. Save the new setting. IMPORTANT! Do not log out during this process! Once the chairperson has removed themselves from their post, you may return to the "Edit Book Details" screen and select a new Chairperson. Once selected, use the "Save" button at the bottom to make the change permanent.
