This is a list of commonly used abbreviations. We recommend that, for the convenience of your readers, you use conventional abbreviations to help aid in understanding the instructions and methods of recipe creation.

For the degree symbol, you may type ^ (shift+6) instead of using the special character drop-down. To easily type fractions, use whole numbers with a slash (i.e. 3/4). They will automatically convert to a fraction when you submit for proof.
